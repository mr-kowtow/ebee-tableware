Our Story

Pioneering Sustainable Tableware

From sugarcane fields to your table — E-BEE Bioproducts has been at the forefront of eco-friendly disposable tableware since 2010.

Home › About Us

E-BEE Bioproducts, a leading manufacturer and supplier of biodegradable disposable tableware. Our company is committed to providing eco-friendly alternatives to traditional disposable tableware, with a focus on sustainability and innovation.

Based in Guangdong, China, E-BEE Bioproducts relies on high technology to produce disposable environmentally friendly tableware and pulp packaging products. Our products replace foamed plastics, promote the control of white pollution, improve human health and living standards, and reduce the spread of diseases.

Our products include pulp food packaging and industrial packaging, cardboard food-grade packaging and bamboo tableware. Product categories include plates, bowls, boxes, straws, cutlery, food bags and industrial product bases.

Our Story

Company Profile

The company's products — all physical properties and hygienic indicators — are in line with the national GB18006.1-1999 standard, and have passed the inspection standards of international health agencies such as the US FDA, the European SGS, and the Japanese Ministry of Health and Welfare.

The company's products have passed the food safety management system (HACCP) certification, ensuring the highest standards of food safety and quality across every product we manufacture.

Company Profile

Equipment innovation leads to breakthrough in production capacity, and stable quality has won the trust of customers. The company has been strictly controlling product quality according to BRC requirements and has strict control over product functions, appearance, and more.
