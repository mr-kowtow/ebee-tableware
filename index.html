About E-BEE

Sustainable Tableware Since 2010

E-BEE Biomaterial is a leading manufacturer of eco-friendly disposable tableware based in Jieyang, Guangdong, China. We specialize in sugarcane bagasse and plant pulp fiber products that are 100% compostable.

We have a comprehensive approach to environmental sustainability that extends beyond the production of biodegradable products — supporting businesses globally to reduce plastic waste.
